About Us
Commenced in the year 2005, with the aim to offer the right value of customers money and emerge as a reputed manufacturer and supplier. Today, we, Air Care Ducting And Insulation feel happy to announce that this dream has come true. We have gained prominence in the market for supplying Air Vent, Axial Fan, Commercial Air Cooler Duct, Commercial GI Duct, etc. Apart from this, we are offering Industrial Evaporative Cooling System Installation, Kitchen & Hotel Cleaning Services, etc. to our esteemed clients. It is the back of our diligent professionals on the basis of which we have been able to stand to gather a wide customer base. Some reputed name to whom we have served till now includes Minda Stoneridge Instruments Ltd, Super Auto India Ltd Nanekarwadi, Indian Card Clothing Company Limited, Lumax Industries Limited, etc. Owing to attributes like clear dealings, easy payment modes, principled business policies, etc. we have gathered a wide customer base. Utilizing the latest CAD technology, sheet metal fabricating equipment, technical competence, we provide our clients with the best quality and service that exceeds their expectations.

We take orders from customers based in Maharashtra & particular Pune, Satara, Solapur, Kolhapur, Ahmednagar, Aurangabad, Nashik & other industrial areas.
